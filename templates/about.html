How to Play

: The Social Trivia Game




  • Join a Troupe on the 'Account' page.
    • Troupes are 'trivia groups'. Members of a troupe are your competitors!

  • Visit this site to answer the daily trivia question.

  • Before answering a question, you must contribute to the troupe's tally.
    • You only have to add 5¢ per day!

  • Answer correctly to earn 2 points.

  • Answer incorrectly to lose 1 point.

  • Question too difficult? You can 'Pass' to earn 1 point.

  • Each month, the highest scorer in each troupe wins a percentage of the tally!

  • To get started, click on Today's Question!
